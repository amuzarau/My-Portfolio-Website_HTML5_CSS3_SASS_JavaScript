My Project #1: Chessboard Vision App

Chessboard Vision App will train chessboard vision - one of underrated skill for each newbie chess player, especially kids. Chessboard vision - ability to visualize chessboard in mind's eye and tell what is the color of each square at the chessboard without looking at the chessboard (for example, "e2 square is black"). Developed chessboard vision skill is one of the most crucial skills for young chess players. This app will help millions of newbie chess players, above all kids, to play chess better and win more often and finally become chess champions.
